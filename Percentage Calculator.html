How to Use This Percentage Calculator

This calculator helps you find the percentage of a number or determine what percentage one number is of another.

To calculate "What is X% of Y?" enter X in the first field and Y in the second field, then click Calculate.

For example, to find 15% of 200, enter 15 in the first field and 200 in the second field.

Percentage Calculation Formulas

What is X% of Y?
Formula: (X / 100) × Y = Result

X is what percent of Y?
Formula: (X / Y) × 100 = Result%

Percentage increase/decrease:
Formula: ((New Value - Original Value) / Original Value) × 100 = % Change
